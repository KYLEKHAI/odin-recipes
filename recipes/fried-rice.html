Fried Rice Recipe

Description

Fried Rice Fried rice is a versatile and delicious dish that originated in Chinese cuisine. It typically consists of cooked rice stir-fried with various ingredients such as vegetables, meat, and sauces, resulting in a flavorful and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

Here are the main ingredients used in this dish:

  • Cooked rice (preferably day-old rice)
  • Vegetables (such as diced carrots, peas, and chopped onions)
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Oil for stir-frying
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

Here are the steps to create the perfect dish:

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium heat.
  2. Add diced onions and cook until they become translucent.
  3. Add the protein of your choice and cook until it's no longer pink and cooked through.
  4. Push the cooked onions and protein to one side of the pan, creating space for the eggs.
  5. Crack the eggs into the pan and scramble them until they're cooked.
  6. Add the diced carrots and any other vegetables you're using, and stir-fry for a few minutes until they're tender.
  7. Add the cooked rice to the pan, breaking up any clumps, and stir-fry everything together.
  8. Season with soy sauce, salt, and pepper, adjusting the quantities to your taste.
  9. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, or chopped green onions for added flavor (optional)
  10. Stir-fry for a few more minutes until everything is well combined and heated through.

Serve hot and enjoy your homemade fried rice!
